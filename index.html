What is Tornado Cash?

Tornado Cash operates as a decentralized mixer for ETH transactions, enabling users to obfuscate the origin of their funds. Unlike traditional financial systems where transactions are traceable, Tornado Cash employs a sophisticated mixing protocol that breaks the link between sender and receiver addresses. This process effectively anonymizes transactions, enhancing user privacy in the blockchain space.
